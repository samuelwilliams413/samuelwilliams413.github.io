# Lemonade
[**Recipes**](recipe.md)
## Materials

|Qty|Ingredient|
|-:|:-|
|1 cp|Lemon Juice|
|1 cp|Boiling water|
|1 cp|Sugar|
|2 cp|Ice|

# Method

1. Stir water, lemon juice, and sugar together till disolved
2. Chill until cool
3. Serve with ice


> Might be worth trying 1 cp of cold water and 1 cp of ice instead
