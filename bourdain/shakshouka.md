# Shakshouka
[**Recipes**](recipe.md)
## Materials

|Qty|Ingredient|
|-:|:-|
|1|Onion, diced|
|1|Capcicum, diced|
|4 cloves|Garlic, minced|
|2 tsp|Paprika|
|1 tsp|Cumin|
|0.25 tsp|Chilli|
|2 cans|Tomato, Diced|
|2 Tbsp|Tomato Paste|
|6|Eggs|
||Coriander|
||Parsley|
||**Cheese**|
||**Bread**|

# Method

1. Brown onion, garlic, paprika, cumin, chilli, and capcicum
2. Add pot, simmer with tomato
3. Add eggs, simmer till cooked
4. Serve with coriander, parlsey, cheese, and bread
