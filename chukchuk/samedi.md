# Samedi

## Attributes

- Fitness
- Intellect
- Wisdom

## Saves
### Body Saves

- Armour
- Constitution

Failed body saves increase **injury** by 1 and may result in a **harm check**.

### Mind Saves

- Fear
- Sanity

Failed mind saves increase **stress** by 1 and may result in a **breakdown check**.
